  • Perfectoid Shimura Varieties of Exceptional Type (In preparation)
    We show that all local and global Shimura varieties at infinite level are perfectoid spaces, including those of exceptional type, verifying a conjecture of Scholze.
  • Compactification of Reductive Group Schemes [pdf] [arXiv]
    We prove that any isotrivial reductive group scheme over an arbitrary base admits a smooth projective equivariant compactification, verifying a conjecture of Česnavičius. In the adjoint case, we recover fiberwise the wonderful compactification. Finally, we give an example of a non-isotrivial torus admitting no equivariant compactification.
